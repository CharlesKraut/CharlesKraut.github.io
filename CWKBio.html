Charles Kraut: A Brief Biography

Born in New York City, Charles grew up in Westchester County, New York. He received his B.A. in Social Sciences from the State University of New York at Stony Brook. Subsequent to his graduation he embarked upon an important but almost unwitting journey of discovery. As a result of his eforts he found and joined the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in 1972.

After four years in the United States Army, Charles spent two years at Brigham Young University and received his MBA degree in Corporate Finance in 1978.

Charles has spent the past 36 years working as a personal financial adviser, serving hundreds of middle-class clients all over the United States. His first book Moneywise: Your Guide to Keeping Ahead of Inflation, Taxes, and the Declining Dollar, published in 2009, was written to help millions of Americans do better financially. Charles continues to publish his quarterly Wealth Creation and Preservation newsletter.

Charles has always been interested in liberty and the Constitution. He got involved with the Constitution Party several years ago, and began speaking to their gatherings and to many other groups.

In connection with his public speaking, Charles wrote and published two more books,

  • The Patriot's Guide to Taking America Back, and
  • Will You Help Save Your Country?

    • Both were written to help conservatives and patriots figure out what has caused America's problems and provide specific solutions.
